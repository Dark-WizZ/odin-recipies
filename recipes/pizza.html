Pizza

Description

Not only is thin crust pizza a delicious meal, but it is also quick to make because you don't have to wait for the dough to rise.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup (177 ml) of lukewarm water
  • 1 tsp (3.15 g) of active, dry yeast
  • 2 cups (256 g) of all-purpose flour, and extra for kneading
  • ¾ tsp (4.27 g) of salt
  • 2 tsp (9.9 mL) of olive oil
  • ½ cup (115 g) of pizza sauce
  • 2 cups (250 g) of shredded mozzarella, parmesan, or romano cheese, or a combination
  • 4 cups of your preferred toppings, such as a combination of sliced onions, mushrooms, red peppers, cooked sausage, or pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 °F (232 °C).
  2. Sprinkle 1 tsp (3.15 g) of yeast over ¾ cup (177 ml) of lukewarm water.
  3. Let the yeast and water stand for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Mix 2 cups (256 g) of flour and ¾ tsp (4.27 g) of salt into the yeast mixture.
  5. Sprinkle a light dusting of flour over a clean work surface.
  6. Knead the dough on the work surface for 5-8 minutes.
  7. Cover the dough with a clean kitchen towel for 10 minutes.
  8. Break the dough into 2 equal parts.
  9. Stretch the dough into 10-12 in (25-30 cm) circles.
  10. Place the rolled dough onto the preheated baking tray.
  11. Spread 1 tsp (4.95 mL) of olive oil over the dough.
  12. Use a knife to spread ¼ cup (57.5 g) of pizza sauce on top of the dough.
  13. Sprinkle on 2 cups of your preferred toppings, and 1 cup (125 g) of cheese.
  14. Bake the pizza in the oven for 8-12 minutes.
  15. Cook the second half of the dough after adding the sauce, toppings, and cheese.
